about me

Hi, I'm Danny Fraley. I'm a student at the University of Richmond Full Stack Coding Boot Camp as well as the Production Manager for an art publishing company in Richmond, World Art Group. I have a background in graphic design and art, with a bachelor's degree from Radford University. I grew up in Richmond and currently live in Chesterfield with my wife, Kate, and our sharpei/beagle pup, Ginger.

In my spare time, I enjoy collecting nerdy poster artwork, watching sports, eating tacos, and pretending to know about beer. I'm an avid movie buff, and would frequently be in a theater at least once a week, pre-COVID. (BOO, COVID!) As a child of the 80's, my house is filled with art and memorabilia featuring some of my favorite films and cartoons from growing up.
